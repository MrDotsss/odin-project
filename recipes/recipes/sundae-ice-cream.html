Sundae Ice Cream

Sprinkled with pretzels, Baileys Ice Cream Sundae recipe is the perfect mix of sweet & salty and creamy & crunchy!

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Baileys Original Irish Cream
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • Pretzels

Directions

  1. Scoop your favorite vanilla ice cream into a dish, add 2 oz. of Baileys Original Irish Cream, smash up pretzels and sprinkle on to top.
